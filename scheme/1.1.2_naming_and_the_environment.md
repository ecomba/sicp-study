# 1.1.2 Naming and the Environment

```scheme
1 ]=> (define size 2)

;Value: size

1 ]=> size

;Value: 2

1 ]=> (* 5 size)

;Value: 10

1 ]=> (define pi 3.14159)

;Value: pi

1 ]=> (define radius 10)

;Value: radius

1 ]=> (* pi (* radius radius))

;Value: 314.159

1 ]=> (define circumference (* 2 pi radius))

;Value: circumference

1 ]=> circumference

;Value: 62.8318

1 ]=>
```
